IT3 Consultants is a consultancy company specialized in the field of Unix (including Linux), disaster recovery, Open Source Projects, Internet and Intranet services.

The name IT3 Consultants was chosen to indicate the three Information Technology domains:

  • Information Technology
  • Interface Technology, and
  • Integration Technology

Our Mission

Our mission is to be the glue between the three IT domains.

Open Source

We are involved in several Open Source projects, such as Relax-and-Recover (ReaR), Relax-and-Recover Automated testing, Upgrade-UX, Config 2 HTML and many more.

We use GIT for version control in all of our projects to ensure that it is easily to collaborate with other developers, experiment with features and control changes. To see all the projects we contribute on have a look on our GitHub page of Gratien D'haese
